'''''Nanotechnology''''' is a [[Peer review|peer-reviewed]] [[scientific journal]] published by [[IOP Publishing]]. It covers research in all areas of [[nanotechnology]]. since 2009, the Editor-in-Chief has been Mark Reed of [[Yale University]], USA.<ref>http://nanotechweb.org/cws/article/yournews/37036</ref>

== Abstracting, indexing, and impact factor ==
According to the ''[[Journal Citation Reports]]'', the journal has a 2012 [[impact factor]] of 3.842.<ref name=WoS>{{cite book |year=2012 |chapter=Nanotechnology |title=2012 [[Journal Citation Reports]] |publisher=[[Thomson Reuters]] |edition=Science |series=[[Web of Science]] |postscript=.}}</ref>
