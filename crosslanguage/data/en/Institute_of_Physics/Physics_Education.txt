'''''Physics Education''''' is a [[Peer review|peer-reviewed]] [[academic journal]] that covers the [[Physics education|teaching of physics]] at the [[secondary school]] and introductory [[undergraduate]] levels. Its scope includes ideas and guidance for classroom teaching, demonstrations and laboratory experiments, international news on [[education]] developments, [[book review]]s, equipment and multimedia products. The [[editor-in-chief]] is Gary Williams. The journal is abstracted and indexed in [[Inspec]] and [[ERIC]]/CIJE.
