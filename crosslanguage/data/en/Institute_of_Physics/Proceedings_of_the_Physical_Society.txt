The '''Proceedings of the Physical Society''' is a journal on the subject of [[physics]], originally associated with the [[Physical Society of London]], [[England]].

{{ISSN|0370-1328}} (Print)

== Journal history ==

* 1874&ndash;1925: ''Proceedings of the Physical Society of London'' 
* 1926&ndash;1948: ''Proceedings of the Physical Society'' 
* 1949&ndash;1957: ''Proceedings of the Physical Society, Section A'' 
* 1949&ndash;1957: ''Proceedings of the Physical Society, Section B''
* 1958&ndash;1967: ''Proceedings of the Physical Society''
