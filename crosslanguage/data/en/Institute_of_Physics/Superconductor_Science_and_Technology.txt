'''''Superconductor Science and Technology''''' is a [[Peer review|peer-reviewed]] [[scientific journal]] covering research on all aspects of [[superconductivity]], including theories of superconductivity, the basic [[physics]] of superconductors, the relation of [[microstructure]] and growth to superconducting properties, the theory of novel devices, and the fabrication and properties of thin films and devices. The [[editor-in-chief]] is D. P. Hampshire ([[University of Durham]]). It was established in 1988 and is  published by [[IOP Publishing]]. According to the ''[[Journal Citation Reports]]'', the journal has a 2012 [[impact factor]] of 2.758.<ref name=WoS>{{cite book |year=2013 |chapter=Superconductor Science and Technology |title=2012 [[Journal Citation Reports]] |publisher=[[Thomson Reuters]] |edition=Science |accessdate=2013-02-08 |series=[[Web of Science]]}}</ref>

==Article types==
The journal publishes articles in the following categories:
* Papers: regular articles reporting original research in superconductivity and its application without formal length restrictions
* Rapid communications: short articles reporting very substantial new advances and no longer than 5 journal pages or 4500 words including figures
* Topical reviews: [[literature review|review papers]] commissioned by the editors
