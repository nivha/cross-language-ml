'''''Nonlinearity''''' is a [[peer-reviewed]] [[scientific journal]] published by [[IOP Publishing]] and the [[London Mathematical Society]]. The journal covers all aspects of [[nonlinear system|nonlinear science]], featuring topics which range from [[physics]], [[mathematics]], and [[engineering]] through to [[biological sciences]]. The [[editors-in-chief]] are Edgar Knobloch ([[University of California, Berkeley]]) and Anatoly I. Neishtadt ([[Loughborough University]] and the [[Russian Space Research Institute]]).
