'''''Journal of Physics D: Applied Physics''''' is a [[peer review|peer-reviewed]] [[scientific journal]] published by [[Institute of Physics|IOP Publishing]], a subsidiary of the [[Institute of Physics]] in the United Kingdom. It has a broad coverage, including five main focus areas: [[magnetism]]; [[photonics]] and [[semiconductor]]s; [[Plasma (physics)|plasmas]] and plasma-surface interactions; applied surfaces and interfaces; and the [[structure]] and properties of [[matter]]. The current [[editor-in-chief]] is Giorgio Margaritondo ([[École Polytechnique Fédérale de Lausanne]]).
