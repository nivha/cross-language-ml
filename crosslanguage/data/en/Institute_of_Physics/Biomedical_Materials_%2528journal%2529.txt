'''''Biomedical Materials''''' is a [[Peer review|peer-reviewed]] [[medical journal]] that covers research on [[tissue engineering]] and [[regenerative medicine]]. The [[Editor-in-chief|editors-in-chief]] are Myron Spector ([[Harvard Medical School]] and [[VA Boston Healthcare System]]) and I.-S. Lee ([[Yonsei University]]).
