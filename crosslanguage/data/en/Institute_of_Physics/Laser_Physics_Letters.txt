'''''Laser Physics Letters''''' is a [[Peer review|peer-reviewed]] [[scientific journal]] established in 2004, that publishes short communications in all area of [[optics]] and applied [[quantum mechanics]], with emphasis on [[laser science]]. The [[editor-in-chief]] is Pavel P. Pashinin ([[A.M. Prokhorov General Physics Institute]]). It was published by [[Wiley-VCH]] until July 2012, when it switched to [[IOP Publishing]].
