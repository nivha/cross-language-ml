Prof. '''Michael Brown''', [[Order of the British Empire|CBE]], [[Deputy Lieutenant|DL]], [[Institute of Physics|FInstP]], [[Institution of Engineering and Technology|FIET]], [[Royal Society of Arts|FRSA]],<ref>http://www.policyreview.tv/conference/410.html</ref> was the 2nd [[Vice-Chancellor]] of [[Liverpool John Moores University]] in [[Liverpool]], [[England]], [[United Kingdom]] <ref>[http://www.ljmu.ac.uk/Governors/64915.htm#Professor%20Michael%20Brown Governors' Biographies<!-- Bot generated title -->]</ref> between 2000 and 2011.

==100th Roscoe Lecture==
In May 2011, Michael Brown delivered the 100th lecture of the [[Liverpool John Moores University]] Roscoe Lectures at the [[Liverpool Philharmonic Hall]], as named after by [[William Roscoe]].<ref>http://www.liverpooldailypost.co.uk/education/education-news/2011/05/16/vice-chancellor-of-liverpool-john-moores-professor-michael-brown-delivers-100th-roscoe-lecture-92534-28698982/</ref>
