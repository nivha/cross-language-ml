The '''''Journal of Statistical Mechanics: Theory and Experiment''''' is a [[Peer review|peer-reviewed]] [[scientific journal]] published by the [[International School for Advanced Studies]] and [[IOP Publishing]]. The journal is targeted to scientists interested in different aspects of [[Statistics|statistical]] [[physics]].<ref name=edit-info>[http://iopscience.org/jstat Journal of Statistical Mechanics: Theory and Experiment] Editorial information.</ref> The [[editor-in-chief]] is Marc Mezard ([[CNRS]] and [[University of Paris-Sud]]).
