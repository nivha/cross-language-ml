==Scope==
The focus of this journal is invited review articles covering all branches of [[physics]]. Each review will typically survey and critique a particular topic, or developments in a field. Introductions of articles are intended for a broad readership, beyond the specialist or expert. In addition to the traditional review article two other formats are available: ''Reports on Progress'' (about 20 pages) and ''Key Issues Reviews'' (about 10 pages).<ref name=scope/>
