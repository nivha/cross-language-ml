'''''New Journal of Physics''''' is an online-only, [[Open access (publishing)|open-access]], [[peer review|peer-reviewed]] [[scientific journal]] covering research in all aspects of [[physics]], as well as [[interdisciplinarity|interdisciplinary]] topics where physics forms the central theme. The journal was established in 1998 and is a joint publication of the [[Institute of Physics]] and the [[Deutsche Physikalische Gesellschaft]]. It is published by [[IOP Publishing]]. The [[editor-in-chief]] is [[Eberhard Bodenschatz]] ([[Max Planck Institute for Dynamics and Self-Organization]]).
