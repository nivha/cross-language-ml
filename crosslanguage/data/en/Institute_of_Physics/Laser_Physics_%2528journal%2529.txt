'''''Laser Physics''''' is a [[Peer review|peer-reviewed]] [[scientific journal]] covering all fundamental and application aspects of [[laser science]]. It was established in 1990 (first printed in 1991) at the initiative of [[Alexander M. Prokhorov]], who served as [[editor-in-chief]] until his death in 2002. The journal was published by [[MAIK Nauka/Interperiodica]] from 1991 to 2005, then by [[Springer Science+Business Media]] from 2006 to 2012.<ref>http://www.lasphys.com/lasphys/#?lponline.php</ref> The current publisher is [[IOP Publishing]], which has published the journal since 2013.<ref name=JISC>[http://www.jisc-collections.ac.uk/UKSG/275/IOP-Publishing-and-Astro-Ltd/?n=121b8a3f-e721-4bc0-8354-7ad4d47f99de UKSG]. Retrieved 2013-09-20</ref> The current editor-in-chief is [[Pavel P. Pashinin]].
