The '''''Journal of Micromechanics and Microengineering''''' is a [[Peer review|peer-reviewed]] [[scientific journal]] that covers all aspects of [[microelectromechanical systems]], devices and structures, as well as [[micromechanics]], microengineering, and [[microfabrication]]. The [[editor-in-chief]] is [[Mark G. Allen]] ([[Georgia Institute of Technology]]).

== Abstracting and indexing ==
The journal had a 2012 [[impact factor]] of 1.790 according to the ''[[Journal Citation Reports]]''. It is indexed in [[Inspec]], [[PASCAL (database)|PASCAL]], [[Current Contents]]/Engineering Computing and Technology, [[Science Citation Index]], [[Chemical Abstracts]], Mass Spectrometry Bulletin, [[Engineering Index]]/[[Compendex]], Applied Mechanics Reviews, and [[VINITI Database RAS]].
