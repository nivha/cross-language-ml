'''''Journal of Radiological Protection''''' is a quarterly [[Peer review|peer-reviewed]] [[scientific journal]] covering [[Radiobiology|radiobiological]] research on all aspects of radiological protection, including [[non-ionizing]] as well as [[ionizing radiation]]s. It is the official journal of the [[Society for Radiological Protection]] and published on their behalf by [[IOP Publishing]]. It was established in 1981 as the ''Journal of the Society for Radiological Protection'', before obtaining its current name in 1988. The [[editor-in-chief]] is Richard Wakeford ([[University of Manchester]]).
