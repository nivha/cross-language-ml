'''Richard Stuart Peckover''' [[FInstP]], [[FIMA]], [[FRMetS]], [[Fellow of the Royal Astronomical Society|FRAS]], [[Safety and Reliability Society|FSaRS]] (5 May 1942 – 15 August 2005) was Chief Safety Adviser to the [[UK Atomic Energy Authority]], 2000–2002.<ref>[http://www.ukwhoswho.com/view/article/oupww/whowaswho/U30431 Peckover, Dr Richard Stuart], ''Who Was Who'', A & C Black, 1920–2008; online edn, [[Oxford University Press]], December 2007. Retrieved 24 May 2011.</ref>

Peckover married [[Carole Jordan]] in 1971; they divorced in 1983. In 1996, he married again to Elizabeth Griffiths (née Richardson).
