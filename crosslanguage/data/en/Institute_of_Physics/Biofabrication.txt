'''''Biofabrication''''' is a [[peer review|peer-reviewed]] [[scientific journal]] covering research that leads to the fabrication of advanced biological models, medical therapeutic products, and non-medical biological systems. The [[editor-in-chief]] is Wei Sun ([[Drexel University]]).
