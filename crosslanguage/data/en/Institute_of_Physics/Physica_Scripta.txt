'''''Physica Scripta''''' is an international [[scientific journal]] for experimental and [[theoretical physics]]. It was established in 1970 and is published jointly by [[IOP Publishing]] on behalf of the [[Royal Swedish Academy of Sciences]] jointly with other Academies of Sciences and Physical Societies of the five [[Nordic Countries]]. The journal covers both experimental and theoretical physics, with an accent on atomic, molecular and optical physics, plasma physics, condensed matter physics and mathematical physics.
