The '''''Journal of Cosmology and Astroparticle Physics''''' is an [[electronic journal|online-only]] [[peer review|peer-reviewed]] [[scientific journal]] focusing on all aspects of [[cosmology]] and [[astroparticle physics]]. This encompasses theory, observation, experiment, computation and simulation. It has been published jointly by [[Institute of Physics|IOP Publishing]] and the [[International School for Advanced Studies]] since 2003.
