The '''Appleton medal and prize''' is awarded for distinguished research in environmental, earth or atmospheric physics.  It was originally called the "Chree medal and prize" after Dr. [[Charles Chree]]<ref name="The Chree Medal and Prize">{{cite web|url=http://www.iop.org/activity/awards/Senior_Awards/The_Charles_Chree_Medal_and_Prize/page_1785.html|title=The Chree Medal and Prize|publisher=Institute of Physics|language=English|accessdate=2 May 2010}}</ref> but was renamed in 2008 by the [[Institute of Physics]].<ref name="Appleton medal and prize">{{cite web|url=http://www.iop.org/about/awards/subject/appleton/page_38518.html|title=Appleton medal and prize|publisher=Institute of Physics|language=English|accessdate=2 May 2010}}</ref> It is currently awarded in even-dated years.
