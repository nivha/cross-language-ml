Hatice Güleryüz (born February 18, 1968) is a contemporary Turkish artist. She has worked in video, photography, art books and drawing.

==Life and work==

Hatice Güleryüz was born in Denizli, Turkey. She has a bachelor's degree in fine art from Dokuz Eylül University, in Izmir, Turkey, and an MA from Plymouth University in England. She did post-graduate work at the Piet Zwart Institute of the Willem de Kooning Academie in Rotterdam, and research at the Jan van Eyck Academie in Maastricht, both in The Netherlands.

Her work has been shown in museums, galleries and festivals in many countries, including at the 9th International Istanbul Biennial, 16 September to 30 October 2005.
