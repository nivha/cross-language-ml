Sinasi Bozatli (*1962) born in Ankara painter, sculptor and graphic artist.

He is a Member of IG Bildende Kunst and IAA / UNESCO. Studied painting and sculpture on Gazi University in Ankara (1984) and painting and graphics on University of Applied Arts in Vienna. He lives and works in Vienna since 1987. He owns studio in Long Island since 1998, partly works in the USA.
