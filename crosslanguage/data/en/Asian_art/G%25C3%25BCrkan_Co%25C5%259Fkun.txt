Gürkan Coşkun (Çorum, 1941 - ) or more widely known as Komet is a famous Turkish painter. After attending 
İstanbul State Academy of Fine Arts between the years 1960 and 1967, he moved to Paris in 1971. Today, he still splits his time between the two cities. His work is exhibited in various countries and is very respected in his home town. In his paintings, he usually blends fantasy and reality.
