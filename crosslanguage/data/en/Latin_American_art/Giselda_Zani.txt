Giselda Zani (b. Italy, 1909 – Argentina, 1975) was a Uruguayan poet, short story writer and art critic of Italian origin. A member of the Generación del 45, he had a career as a journalist and diplomat in Buenos Aires in Argentina.

==Works==
* La costa despierta (The Wide-awake Coast) (1930)
* Por vínculos sutiles (Tenuous Links) (1958), a collection of short stories

== Awards ==
* Emecé Literary Prize (1957)
