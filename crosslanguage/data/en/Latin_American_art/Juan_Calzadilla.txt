Juan Calzadilla (Altagracia de Orituco, 1931) is a Venezuelan prolific poet, painter and art critic.

He studied in the  Universidad Central de Venezuela and in the Instituto Pedagógico Nacional. He is co-founder of the group  El techo de la ballena (1961) and Imagen magazine (1984).

==Works==
*Dictado por la jauría (1962)
*Malos modales (1968)
*Oh smog (1978)
*Antología paralela (1988)
*Minimales (1993)
*Principios de Urbanidad (1997)
*Corpolario (1998)
*Diario sin sujeto (1999)
*Aforemas (2004)

==Awards==
