Rafael Pérez Barradas (Montevideo, 4 January 1890 - 12 February 1929) was a Uruguayan painter and artist.

==Biography==
He was born with the name of Rafael Manuel Pérez Giménez Barradas Roja and was the brother of composer Carmen Barradas and poet Antonio De Ignacios.

His career was short but intense; he left most of his paintings in Spain.

He is buried at Buceo Cemetery.
