After the Software Wars book written by Keith Curtis, a former Microsoft employee who worked for the company from 1993 to 2004, which is free software and its importance in the computing industry, specifically its impact on Microsoft's and software propietario.En book discusses the importance of free and shared knowledge and sense of community. It also provides examples and challenges both free software and knowledge resources as Linux and Wikipedia.
