In computer science, specifically in the area of computational complexity, Computers and Intractability: A Guide to the Theory of NP-Completeness is an influential textbook written by Michael Garey and David S. Johnson.Fue the first book to treat formally NP-completeness and intractability. The book contains an appendix that provides a comprehensive compendium of NP-completeness problems, which has been updated in reprints of the book.
