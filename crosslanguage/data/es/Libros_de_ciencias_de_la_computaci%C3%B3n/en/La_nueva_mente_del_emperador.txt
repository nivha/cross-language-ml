The Emperor's New Mind is a 1989 book written by prominent mathematical physicist Roger Penrose.Penrose defends the argument that human consciousness is non-algorithmic, and therefore can not be modeled by a Turing machine or digital computers. The author points out the hypothesis that quantum mechanics plays an essential role in the understanding of consciousness.
