Omar Varela is an actor, director, teacher and playwright Uruguayan, founder of the theater company Fausta.Se Italy settled in Brazil, having been part of the regular cast of the National Comedy. Multidisciplinary Graduate School of Dramatic Arts in Montevideo. He directed plays and musicals like "My Fair Lady", "Opera do Malandro", "Who's Afraid of Fausta Italy?" (in the lineup for more than 10 years). He was also one of the founding pioneers of theater for children at the Iris Prize Uruguay.Ganador.
