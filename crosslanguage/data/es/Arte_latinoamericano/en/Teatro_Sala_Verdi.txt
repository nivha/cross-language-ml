The Teatro Sala Verdi, is a Uruguayan art scene, is located in the street Soriano 914, Barrio Sur, Montevideo. Founded in 1894. == History == The construction effort was Uruguayan musicians and Luis Francisco Sambucetti, on 5 September 1890 founded the Institute Verdi. The design of the British engineer John Adams, Neoclassical received modifications in 1910, 1954 and between 2007 and 2009.En the main facade are busts of Giuseppe Verdi and Richard Wagner.En 1946 it became the property of the Municipality Montevideo is one of the headquarters of the National Comedy, official stable cast of Uruguay, founded in 1949.
