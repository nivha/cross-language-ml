Pedro and the Captain is a theatrical drama written by the Uruguayan Mario Benedetti. It was published by Editorial Santillana in 1979. == Summary == "Peter and the captain." It is a play in four acts, is based on the dialogue between the characters Pedro the tortured, and Captain the torturer, possibly located at the time of Argentina's military dictatorship of 1976, even in the play, never say accurately where elapses.
