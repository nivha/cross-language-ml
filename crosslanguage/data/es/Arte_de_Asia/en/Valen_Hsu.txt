His songs are mostly cantandos are in Mandarin, Cantonese, with some exceptions. == Biography == Valen began playing the piano at age 4 years, and followed a classical training. It was discovered in 1993 during the years of school, while working at a restaurant in Taipei. He was then offered a contract with Taiwan What's Music Label, where he began recording his first commercial album at age 19 == Discography == * Giugno 1995 -.
