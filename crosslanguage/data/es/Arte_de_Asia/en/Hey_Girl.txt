Hey Girl is a Taiwanese Mandopop girl group. They were formed when the company organized the hearings if Wo Ai Hei Hui (我 爱 黑 涩 会), a variety show on television program aimed at creating new female characters of the series in Taiwan. In late 2005, the V Channel nine members elected as representatives of the girls participating in the program. These nine members were Gui Gui, Xiao Jie, Mei Mei, Apple, Xiao Xun, Ya Tou, Xiao Man, Ya Tong Tong and Da.
