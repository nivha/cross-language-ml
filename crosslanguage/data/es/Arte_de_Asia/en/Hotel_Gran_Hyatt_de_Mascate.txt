The Grand Hyatt Muscat Hotel is a luxury 5 star Shaati Al Qurum, Muscat, the capital of the Asian country of Oman. The hotel is often used for diplomatic conferences. The hotel has 280 rooms and is located on 10 acres (40,000 square meters). It is designed in traditional Arabian style architecture and has a high window with a window 14 meters overlooking the pool. The hotel has 4 restaurants and 2 bars including the Italian restaurant La Toscana, the Sirj Tea Lounge, Grill House, the Mokha Café and Café Habana.
