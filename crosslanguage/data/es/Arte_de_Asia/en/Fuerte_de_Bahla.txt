Bahla Fort (Arabic: قلعة بهلاء; Qal Bahla ') is one of four forts at the foot of Djebel Akhdar, in the upper part of Oman, is housed in the oasis of Bahla. It was inscribed on the World Heritage Site by UNESCO in the year 1987.Fue community built by Banu Nabhan who ruled the region from the twelfth century to the late fifteenth century.
