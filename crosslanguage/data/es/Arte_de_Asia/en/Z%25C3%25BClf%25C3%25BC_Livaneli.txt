Ömer Zülfü Livaneli (ilgin, Turkey, 1946) is a Turkish singer and songwriter of folk music as well as political editor, novelist and filmmaker. This intellectual left was a parliamentary term (2002-2007) under the colors of the Republican People's Party (CHP), abandon party in 2005 as his trial was a chauvinist and undemocratic change his party.
