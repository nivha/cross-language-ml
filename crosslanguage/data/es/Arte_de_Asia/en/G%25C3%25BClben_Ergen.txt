Gülben Ergen (born August 25, 1972) is a Turkish singer and actress. Born in Istanbul, Turkey, and has acted in films and on television. Her most famous role is probably that of Melek in Dadi, the Turkish version of The Nanny. She was named Miss Cinema 1987.
