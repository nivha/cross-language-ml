Pinhani is a soft-rock band formed by cousins Sinan Kaynakçı (vocals and rhythm guitar) and Zeynep Eylül Üçer (vocals and bass). The other active members of the band are leader Akın Eldes guitar, and in Hami Ünlü battery. == Discography == His debut album, "İnandığın Masallar" (fairy tales in which think) was released in April 2006 and was the great success that established the band. Their sound is unique, consists of sweet tones, soft guitar solos, and very clean vocals and claras.Lanzaron their second album "Beklemez Zaman" (Time and Tide) in April 2008 === 2006 -.
