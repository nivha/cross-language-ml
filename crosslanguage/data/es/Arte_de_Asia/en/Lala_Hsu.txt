miniaturadeimagenLala Hsu (Chinese: 徐佳莹, Simplified Chinese: 徐佳莹, Pinyin: Xu Jiaying), (born December 20, 1984, Taichung), is a Taiwanese singer and songwriter. Won the "Golden Melody" award in 2010, nominated as the best interpreter of his home country and was also crowned after participating in a singing competition at the event called "One Million Star". He has released three albums in his career. Among his best-known albums are sold and Limits (极限) 2010 and Ideal Life (理想 人生) 2012.
