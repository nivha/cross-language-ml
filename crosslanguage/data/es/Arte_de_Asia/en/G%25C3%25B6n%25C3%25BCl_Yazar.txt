Gönül Yazar (maiden name: Gönül Özyeğiner), born in the port city of Izmir on December 8, 1936, is a popular Turkish singer and actress. It is one of the most famous artists of Turkish varieties of all time. Its popularity reached its peak during the 1960s and 1970s.
