The Festival Song Turkvisión (Turkish Türkvyzion şarkı) is an annual televised competition in which countries and regions of Turkic or Turkic minority peoples, having similar characteristics as the Eurovision Song Contest. Created in 2013 by the chain of Turkish public television TRT in order to promote the Turkic culture.
