Sam Lee (traditional Chinese: 李圣杰; simplified Chinese: 李圣杰, pinyin: Lǐ, Sichuan, Wade-Giles: Lee Sheng-chieh, Pe-OE-ji: Li Seng Kiat), born February 21, 1973, is a singer and Taiwanese composer. He is the grandson of an immigrant of German descent by the mother.
