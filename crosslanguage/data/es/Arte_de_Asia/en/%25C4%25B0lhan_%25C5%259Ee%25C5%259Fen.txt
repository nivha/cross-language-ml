Ilhan sixty (Manisa, June 18, 1948) is a Turkish singer and actor. He began playing music at age 20, in 1968. In 1972 he released his first album, called Kavga. In 1983 he founded together with the sixty Gökhan and Burhan Gündoğarken group. In 2002 he released his second solo album, Neler oluyor bize. In that year he began to combine his musical career with acting for television. == External links ==
