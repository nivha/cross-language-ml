Cherry Boom (Traditional Chinese: 樱桃 帮, Simplified Chinese: 樱桃 帮, Pinyin: Yingtao Bang) is a Taiwanese rock group consisting of four girls, students at Fu Jen Catholic University. They write and play their own music.
