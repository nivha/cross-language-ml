Wu Tsing-Fong (Chinese: 吴青峰, Simplified Chinese: 吴青峰, pinyin: WU Qingfeng, born August 8, 1982) is a Taiwanese singer-songwriter and lead singer of the indie band Sodagreen, also one Member and founder. He has collaborated with other artists such as Angela Zhang, Eason Chan and Rainie Yang.
