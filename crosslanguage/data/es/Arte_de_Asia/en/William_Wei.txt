William Wei (also known as Wei Li An) (March 5, 1987, Taichung), is a Taiwanese singer and composer. He attended the "Dali Senior High School" and graduated overseas department of National Taiwan University and language in 2010. Their parents, Chin Wei-long (韦金龙) and Chen Yu-mei (陈玉 美) are professors in the departments of foreign languages at the National Chung Hsing University and National Chung Cheng.La University first began publishing his music blog Streetvoice, placed online platform in 2006, which received attention of the media in what was considered the champion in the CTS television network in the "Happy Sunday" or "Happy Sunday," (快乐 星期天) program.
