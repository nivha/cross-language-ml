林逸欣 (Lin Yi Xin Wu, November 5, 1985), better known as Shara Lin Yixin or Shara Lin Yixin Lin is a Taiwanese actress and singer. Mainly plays the violin and piano, but also the guzheng and guitar; is multilingual (Chinese, Korean, Filipino, English and Japanese) and is developed as a model and TV host. == History == At 6 years starred in his first film. As a violinist, at age 12 he received a gold medal at an international competition in Japan. He made his first starring role in the movie Summer Times. == References ==
