Fazıl Say (Ankara, January 14, 1970) is a Turkish pianist and composer known worldwide. He is the son of Ahmet Say, a prominent musicologist and writer turco.thumb | 200px | Fazıl Say 2011 == Biography == Fazıl Say, born in Ankara (Turkey), he studied piano and composition at the Ankara State Conservatory. At seventeen, he was awarded a scholarship from the German Academic Exchange Service enabled him to study for five years with David Levine at the Robert Schumann Institute in Düsseldorf (Germany). From 1992 to 1995 he continued his studies at the Berlin Conservatory.
