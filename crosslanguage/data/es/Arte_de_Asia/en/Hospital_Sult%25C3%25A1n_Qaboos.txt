The Sultan Qaboos Hospital is 450 hospital beds in the secondary capital of Dhofar, the city of Salalah, Oman. It is under the administrative control of the Ministry of Health of Oman. Features multidisciplinary departments including medicine, surgery, pediatrics, obstetrics and gynecology, otolaryngology, ophthalmology, dermatology and dental surgery. It is staffed by physicians from India, Bangladesh, Pakistan, Egypt, Sudan, Oman, among others.
