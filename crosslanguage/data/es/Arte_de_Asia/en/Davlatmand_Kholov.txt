Davlatmand Kholov is a musician and singer of Tajikistan, an expert in the ancient style of music Tajik "Falak". He is known for his performances in two dutar instrument strings and the ghijak instruments of Central Asia. Plays and sings Sufi poetry, mostly Jalaleddin Rumi.
