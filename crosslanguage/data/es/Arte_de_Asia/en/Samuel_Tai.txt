miniaturadeimagen | TaiSamuel Samuel Tai (Chinese: 邰正宵, pinyin: Tai Zhengxiao) (born November 6, 1966, Hong Kong) is a Taiwanese singer and composer. Samuel Tai is also known as the "Prince of Roses" or "The Prince" of the song. He currently lives in Taipei, but also obtains residence in other countries such as Hong Kong and mainland China. Famous for its good looks, vocal talent and composition, Samuel began his career in 1988 and then released his first solo debut entitled "Good Boys" (理想 男孩) 1990 album. In 1993, he wrote a song titled "999 Roses" (九百 九 拾 九朵 玫瑰), which proved a great success. His best known songs include as Thousand Origami Cranes (千纸鹤) 1001 Nights (一千零一夜 [2]) and Let You Hear My Heart Beating (心 要让 你 听见), among others. He has subsequently released 30 albums during his career. His dedication and love for music made him continue, compose and sing their own written and composed by the same songs.
