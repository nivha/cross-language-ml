Idil Biret (Angora, Turkey, November 21, 1941) is a Turkish-born pianist, renowned for her interpretations of the Romantic repertoire pieces. == Biography == İdil Biret Born in Ankara in 1941. Started lessons at age five fenmen with Mithat, who had studied with Nadia Boulanger and Alfred Cortot. When he was seven the Turkish Parliament passed a special law that allowed him to study abroad. He then began his studies at the Paris Conservatory under the tutelage of Nadia Boulanger. Madeleine Valmarète introduced him to composers like Brahms, who later would record his complete works. He graduated with three awards at age 15. He continued his training with Alfred Cortot and Wilhelm Kempff. === === Musical path since sixteen years has performed as a soloist in the most distinguished orchestras around the world, including the London Symphony, the London Philharmonic, the Boston Symphony, St. Petersburg Philharmonic, the Gewandhaus Orchestra of Leipzig, the Saxon State Orchestra Dresden, Orchestre de la Suisse Romande, the Tokyo Philharmonic, the Warsaw Philharmonic, the Orchestre National de France and the Sydney Symphony. He has collaborated with eminent conductors such as Hermann Scherchen, Pierre Monteux, Erich Leinsdorf, Rudolf Kempe, Gennady Rozhdestvensky, Aaron Copland, Rafael Frühbeck de Burgos, Charles Mackerras, Jean Fournet, Moshe Atzmon, Antoni Wit and Hiroyuki Iwaki. Biret has also performed at festivals in Berlin, Montreal, Istanbul, Dubrovnik, Montpellier, Nohant-Vic, Persepolis, Royan and Atenas.Idil Biret has played in cycles all the piano works of Beethoven and Brahms. In the 1980s starred in two series of concerts of the 32 Beethoven sonatas and the piano transcriptions by Liszt made nine symphonies, these will úlimas were issued live on Radio France. In the 1990s he played the five piano concertos and the Choral Fantasy Beethoven Triple Concerto five times. In 1997 addressed the complete works for solo piano by Brahms in a series of five recitals in Germany to coincide with the centennial anniversary músico.Asimismo, has been a member of the jury of many piano competitions: Van Cliburn U.S. Queen Elisabeth of Belgium , Montreal Canada, Busoni and Liszt Weimar Italy. === === recordings Biret has recorded with labels like EMI, Decca, Atlantic, Finnadar, Naxos. These recordings are the first recording made by Liszt transcriptions of the nine Beethoven symphonies in 1986, the complete solo works of Chopin and concerts in 1992 of Brahms in 1997, Rachmaninoff in 2000. Transcripts also ballet music as the Firebird by Stravinsky in 2003. Their repertoire covers much of the classical and romantic piano literature. Probably best known for his recordings of Chopin. In 2004 the sales of their albums with Naxos worldwide reached two million copies, making Naxos awarded a platinum disc. His classic recordings of the twentieth century include sonatas for piano Pierre Boulez and the piano works and editions of Wilhelm Kempff. Also recorded studies (vol. 1 and 2) of György Ligeti released in 2003. During the decade of the 70 recorded works Boucourechliev, Anton Webern, Alban Berg, Mimaroglu, Leo Brouwer, Castiglioni, Alexander Scriabin, Sergei Prokofiev and Nikolai Myaskovsky for Finnadar / Atlantic label in New York.Recientemente recorded the complete Beethoven sonatas (including the choral Fantasy) for his own record IBA; his work has been published as a collection along with the nine Beethoven symphonies transcribed for piano by Liszt. Biret recorded most of the sonatas near Brussels, between 2001 and 2005, while three were recorded in 1994. == == Prizes and Awards Since 1970 Biret has been recognized as "National Artist" (Devlet Sanatçısı), an honorary title awarded to artists by the government of Turkey for its contribution to the Turkish culture. In 1995 his recordings of Chopin were awarded the "Grand Prix du Disque Frédéric Chopin" in Poland. That same year he won the French prize "Diapason d'Or" (Diapason d'Or) by recordings of Boulez's sonatas, selected as the best of the year according to the French newspaper Le Monde.Además Biret has received other awards such as: the prize "Lily Boulanger Memorial" in Boston, the "Gold Medal Harriet Cohen / Dinu Lipatti" in London, the Polish Artistic Merit award for the recognition as "Knight of the National Order of Merit" from France, "Adelaide Ristori award" in Italy.
