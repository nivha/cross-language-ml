Leonid Osipovich Utyosov or Utesov (Леонид Осипович Утёсов); whose real name is Lazar (Leyzer) Vaysbeyn or Weissbein (Russian: Лазарь (Лейзер) Иосифович Вайсбейн) (March 21, 1895, Odessa - 9 March 1982, Moscow) was a famous singer of Soviet jazz and comedian of Jewish origin, who became the first pop singer to be awarded the prestigious title of People's Artist of the USSR in 1965 == Filmography == * 1919 - Lieutenant Schmidt - Freedom Fighter (Russian:.
